About Game

Example Game is an exciting new title from Earwax Games that challenges players to explore a vibrant world full of puzzles and adventures. With its unique blend of strategy and action, Example Game offers an unforgettable gaming experience for players of all skill levels.

Features

  • Immersive storyline with branching narratives
  • Challenging puzzles that test your problem-solving skills
  • Beautiful, hand-crafted environments to explore
  • Dynamic combat system with a variety of weapons and abilities
  • Customizable character progression
  • Multiple endings based on your choices

Release Date and Updates

Example Game is set to release on September 15, 2023, for PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X/S. We're committed to providing regular updates and expansions to keep the game fresh and exciting. Here's our planned update schedule:

  • October 2023: New Game+ mode and additional cosmetic items

  • February 2024: Major expansion adding a new playable area and storyline
  • Ongoing: Regular balance updates and bug fixes based on community feedback
